Featured Artists Section

featured artists

Mark Millar

Civil War, Wanted, Kick Ass, Huck...

Mark Millar is one of the most acclaimed and successful authors of current American comics, writer of some of the most critical and popular series of recent years.

Jim Starlin

Thanos, The Infinity Gauntlet, The Infinity War...

Jim Starlin is an American comic book writer and cartoonist. He is mainly known for being the creator of Thanos.

Alan Moore

V for Vendetta, Watchmen, From Hell...

Alan Moore is a British writer and comic book writer and has been listed as the best graphic novel writer in history.

Frank Miller

Born Again, Sin City, 300, Elektra...

Frank Miller is one of the most influential authors in world comics, known above all for having redefined Daredevil and Batman during the 1980s.

Humberto Ramos

Spider-man, Runaways, Extraordinary X-men...

Humberto Ramos is a Mexican comic book penciller, best known for his work on American comic books and his creator-owned series Crimson.

Brian Michael Bendis

Civil War II, World War Hulk, Secret Invasion...

Brian Michael Bendis is an American comic book writer and artist. He has won five Eisner Awards for both his creator-owned work and his work on various Marvel Comics books.
